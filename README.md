# 📊 Insurance Claims & Policy Analysis Dashboard (PRISM Pvt. Limited)

## 🔍 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing insurance data to generate meaningful insights related to policy performance, claim trends, and customer demographics. The dashboard helps stakeholders understand business performance and improve decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

* Analyze premium and coverage distribution across policy types
* Understand claim status trends (Rejected, Settled, Pending)
* Identify high-risk customer segments based on age and policy type
* Track active vs inactive policies
* Evaluate claim amounts across different demographics

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used

* Microsoft Power BI
* Data Cleaning & Transformation
* Data Modeling
* DAX (for calculated measures)

---

## 📊 Key Metrics (KPIs)

* **Total Premium Amount:** 5.98M
* **Total Coverage Amount:** 600.55M
* **Total Claim Amount:** 16.91M

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### 🔹 Policy Analysis

* Premium Amount by Policy Type (Travel, Health, Auto, Life, Home)
* Coverage distribution insights

### 🔹 Claims Analysis

* Claim Status Breakdown (Rejected, Settled, Pending)
* Claim Amount by Age Group (Adult, Elder, Young Adult)

### 🔹 Customer Insights

* Gender-wise distribution
* Customer-level filtering (Customer ID, Policy Number, Claim Number)

### 🔹 Policy Activity

* Active vs Inactive Policy Distribution

---

## 💡 Key Insights

* Travel policies generate the highest premium revenue
* A significant portion of claims are rejected, indicating possible risk or fraud patterns
* Adult age group contributes the highest claim amount
* Active policies dominate over inactive ones
* Certain policy types have higher settlement rates
